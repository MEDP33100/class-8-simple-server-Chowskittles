Welcome to Cherry Blossom Spring!

Cherry blossoms, or sakura, are a symbol of renewal and the fleeting nature of life in Japanese culture. They bloom for only a short period each spring, creating breathtaking landscapes.

In New York City, you can enjoy cherry blossoms at the Brooklyn Botanic Garden, Central Park, and Flushing Meadows-Corona Park. The annual Sakura Matsuri festival at the Brooklyn Botanic Garden is a popular event celebrating Japanese culture and the beauty of cherry blossoms.
